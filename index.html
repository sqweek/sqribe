About

Sqribe aims to assist with the process of musical transcription, the task of listening to a music recording and determining which notes were played. Sqribe makes it easy to focus on small sections of music at a time, and allows you to listen to your notated music in time with the original recording. These features make transcription more accessible; with some trial and error you can often determine the notes without requiring a masterful musical ear.
